Racine Station Reopens; Enter and Exit via Loomis Street, Only

How does this affect my trip?
The Racine Blue Line station will reopen with the resumption of Blue Line service through the area, however the Racine Avenue entrance to the station will remain closed as we build a new, accessible station building in its place.

To access the Racine Blue Line station, use the Loomis Street entrance/exit, instead, 2 blocks west.

The work at the Racine station is part of the Forest Park Rebuild project and the new Racine Avenue entrance is scheduled to open in late 2024.
