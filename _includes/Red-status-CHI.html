Red and Purple Line Trains Share Track between Thorndale and Belmont (Updated)

How does this affect my trip? 
Red Line trains and Purple Line Express trains are sharing the same tracks between Thorndale and Belmont.

Purple Line Express trains continue to run express between Howard and Belmont, making only regular stops at Howard, Wilson and Belmont.

Note: At the same time, Berwyn and Lawrence are temporarily closed and temporary stations are open at Bryn Mawr and Argyle, to support reconstruction work. See separate alerts for details.

Allow extra travel time.

Why is service being changed? 
Red and Purple line service is changed due to the temporary closure of the two east tracks between Bryn Mawr and Wilson stations, and the two west tracks between Addison and Belmont stations, for reconstruction as part of the Red and Purple Modernization (RPM) project.

Bus Substitution Between Addison and Belmont Stations

How does this affect my trip?
Shuttle buses replace rail service between Addison and Belmont.

Trains operate between Howard and Addison, and between Belmont, Downtown and 95th.

Allow extra travel time.

Why is service being changed?
We are performing track construction to open the second of two newly-reconstructed west tracks, and temporarily close the two east tracks for reconstruction, between Belmont and Addison stations as part of the Red and Purple Modernization (RPM) project.

Map

Bus shuttle boarding locations
Station Direction Shuttle stop
Addison toward Belmont station on Addison, north side, in front of the Addison station main entrance (at westbound #152 stop)
Belmont toward Addison station on Belmont, south side, in front of the Belmont station main entrance (at eastbound #77 stop)
