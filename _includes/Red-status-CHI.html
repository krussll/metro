Red and Purple Line Trains Share Track between Thorndale and Belmont (Updated)

How does this affect my trip? 
Red Line trains and Purple Line Express trains are sharing the same tracks between Thorndale and Belmont.

Purple Line Express trains continue to run express between Howard and Belmont, making only regular stops at Howard, Wilson and Belmont.

Note: At the same time, Berwyn and Lawrence are temporarily closed and temporary stations are open at Bryn Mawr and Argyle, to support reconstruction work. See separate alerts for details.

Allow extra travel time.

Why is service being changed? 
Red and Purple line service is changed due to the temporary closure of the two east tracks between Bryn Mawr and Wilson stations, and the two west tracks between Addison and Belmont stations, for reconstruction as part of the Red and Purple Modernization (RPM) project.

Red Line Subway Trains Rerouted to Elevated Tracks

Red Line subway trains are rerouted to the elevated lines between Fullerton and Cermak-Chinatown due to police activity.

We apologize for the inconvenience and are working to restore service as quickly as possible. Allow extra travel time.

Red Line service to 95th/Dan Ryan

After stopping at Fullerton, 95th-bound trains will be diverted and will make the following elevated station stops:

  • Armitage (accessible)
  • Sedgwick (accessible)
  • Chicago (at Franklin; accessible)
  • Merchandise Mart (accessible)
  • Clark/lake (accessible)
  • State/Lake
  • Washington/Wabash (accessible)
  • Adams/Wabash
  • Roosevelt (elevated station west of Wabash; accessible)

Then, trains will stop at Cermak-Chinatown and make all normal stops to 95th/Dan Ryan.

Red Line service to Howard

For service toward Howard, after stopping at Cermak-Chinatown, trains will then be diverted to the above-listed elevated stations, in the opposite order, through Armitage and then make all normal stops from Fullerton to Howard.

For service in areas away from where the Red Line is running

Consider using local bus alternatives through the area where you're traveling, including those to other train lines or where Red Line service has been rerouted to operate.
