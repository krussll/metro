Red and Purple Line Trains Share Track between Thorndale and Belmont (Updated)

How does this affect my trip? 
Red Line trains and Purple Line Express trains are sharing the same tracks between Thorndale and Belmont.

Purple Line Express trains continue to run express between Howard and Belmont, making only regular stops at Howard, Wilson and Belmont.

Note: At the same time, Berwyn and Lawrence are temporarily closed and temporary stations are open at Bryn Mawr and Argyle, to support reconstruction work. See separate alerts for details.

Allow extra travel time.

Why is service being changed? 
Red and Purple line service is changed due to the temporary closure of the two east tracks between Bryn Mawr and Wilson stations, and the two west tracks between Addison and Belmont stations, for reconstruction as part of the Red and Purple Modernization (RPM) project.

Howard-bound Subway Trains Rerouted to Elevated Tracks

How does this affect my trip?
Howard-bound Red Line subway trains are rerouted to the elevated tracks between Cermak-Chinatown and Fullerton (see map below).

95th-bound Red Line subway trains will continue to serve stations through the State Street Subway.

At Roosevelt station:

  • Board/exit Howard-bound trains on the Green/Orange lines elevated platform.
  • For 95th-bound service, board in subway. 

Downtown:

  • Board/exit Howard-bound trains at any Loop elevated station along Wabash or Lake.
  • For 95th-bound service, board in subway.

At Grand, Chicago, Clark/Division, and North/Clybourn stations:

  • For service to these stations, take a Howard-bound train to Fullerton and transfer to a 95th-bound train.
  • For service from these stations, take a 95th-bound train to Lake and transfer to a Howard-bound train.
  • For 95th-bound service, board in subway.

Allow extra travel time.

Why is service being changed?
We are performing track maintenance in the State Street Subway.

*Entry/exit at Merchandise Mart station is via the Kinzie auxiliary entrance stairways, and requires a farecard (no vending machines), from 2am to 4am Saturday and 2am to 5am Sunday. ADA-accessible entry/exit not available during these times.
