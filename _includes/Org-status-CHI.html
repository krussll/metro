Orange Line Delays, Limited Service Between Western and Midway

Orange Line service experiencing delays due to track conditions; limited service via train shuttle available between Western and Midway.

As a result, trains are operating between Western and Midway, only.  

Board shuttle buses at Midway for continuing service to the Loop, downtown.

See station personnel for further assistance, and pay attention to announcements on trains and at stations for service information.

We are working to restore service as quickly as possible. Allow extra travel time.

No Trains Between 35th/Archer and Ashland

Orange Line service has been temporarily suspended between 35th/Archer and Ashland  due to an earlier derailment.

As a result, trains are operating in two sections:

  • between Midway and 35th/Archer, and,
  • between Ashland and the Loop, downtown, only.

Shuttle buses are available between Midway and Adams/Wabash to provide connecting service.

At this time, also consider other service alternatives that may serve your trip, such as nearby bus routes, or using buses to other rail lines.

These may include the #62 Archer bus.

See station personnel for further assistance, and pay attention to announcements on trains and at stations for service information.

We are working to restore service as quickly as possible. Allow extra travel time.
